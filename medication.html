Medication Safety Tips

Store Properly

Keep medicines in a cool, dry place and out of reach of children.

Follow Dosage

Always take the prescribed amount at the right time. Do not double doses.

Check Expiry Dates

Never use expired medication. Dispose of old medicines safely.

Consult Your Doctor

If you miss a dose or feel unwell, contact your healthcare provider.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I set reminders for multiple medicines?

Yes, you can keep a list and set reminders for each medicine you take.

What if I miss a dose?

Do not double your next dose. Follow your doctor's advice or check the leaflet.

How do I dispose of old medication?

Return unused or expired medicines to your pharmacy for safe disposal.
