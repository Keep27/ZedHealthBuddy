Wellness Tips

Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily.

Be Active

Stay active with 30 minutes of exercise.

Eat Well

Eat fruits and vegetables daily.

Rest

Get at least 7-8 hours of sleep.

Relax

Practice stress management and relaxation.

Healthy Living Tips

Wash Hands

Wash your hands regularly to prevent illness.

Limit Sugar

Reduce sugary drinks and snacks for better health.

Protect from Mosquitoes

Use nets and repellents to avoid malaria.
